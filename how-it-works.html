Simple & Easy

How It Works

We've made the laundry experience as effortless as possible just 4 simple steps and you're done.

1

Schedule Your Pickup

Book online in under 2 minutes. Choose your pickup date, time, and preferred services. We'll confirm within the hour.

2

We Pick Up

Our driver arrives at your doorstep at your chosen time. Pack your laundry in any bag and we'll handle the rest.

3

We Clean

Your order is logged, tagged, and processed separately. Washed, dried, and folded to perfection using premium detergents.

4

Delivered to You

Your clean laundry is neatly packed and delivered back to your door fresh, folded, and ready to go.

What to Expect

Every Order is Labeled

We tag each bag with a unique order ID. Your clothes are never mixed with another customer's ever.

Premium Detergents

We use commercial-grade, hypoallergenic, biodegradable detergents that are safe for sensitive skin and the environment.

Fabric-Specific Care

Delicates, darks, whites, and coloreds are sorted and washed at the correct temperature for each fabric type.

Quality Check

Before packing, every item is inspected for stains, damage, or missing pieces. Only perfect clothes leave our facility.

Neat & Packed

Folded clothes are packed flat in sealed bags to stay fresh and wrinkle-free during delivery.

Order Tracking

You'll receive SMS updates at every stage pickup confirmed, cleaning started, out for delivery.
